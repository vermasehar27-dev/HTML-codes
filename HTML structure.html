What is coding?

Coding, or programming, is writing step-by-step instructions (code) in a specific language (like Python or JavaScript) that tells a computer or device how to perform tasks, build websites, create apps, analyze data, and power virtually all modern technology by translating human ideas into machine-readable commands. It's essentially communicating with computers, giving them a precise "recipe" to follow to achieve a desired outcome.
